About The Rally Club

"More than a match — it’s a community built by players, for players."

Justin Jade Cabrera

2nd Year BSIT Student, AdDU | 19 Years Old

This website was created as a final project for my Web Systems and Technology course. I am both a badminton athlete and a programmer, and I wanted to combine these two passions into a single project. I thought it would be fun to build something for a community I'm actually a part of. As an avid badminton player, I recognized the challenges of finding playmates and organizing games. This inspired me to develop The Rally Club, a platform designed to connect players and facilitate game scheduling within the badminton community.

Our Mission

The Rally Club was started to solve one simple problem: finding people to play badminton with. Our mission is to connect players, make scheduling games easier, and build a strong local community for everyone who loves the sport, from beginners to advanced players.

The Project

This website is a prototype built using only **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**. There is no server or main database. All "created" games and posts are stored in simple arrays and will reset when the page is reloaded. It's designed to be a lightweight, easy-to-understand demonstration of the core features.

Core Features

  • Create a Queue: Easily set up a new game by providing a location, date, time, price, and skill level.
  • Join a Game: Browse all available games created by other players and join their queue with a single click.
  • Connect: A simple, text-only feed to share your thoughts, look for partners, or post about your latest match.
